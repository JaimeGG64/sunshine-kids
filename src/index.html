About Us

The Sunshine Kids Foundation provides exciting, positive group activities and opportunities for children with cancer so they may once again do what kids are meant to do... have fun and celebrate life. We provide exciting, positive group activities and opportunities

Our Programs

National Trips
National trips are week-long trips provided throughout the year for teenage cancer patients from hospitals across the United States and Canada.
Local Activities
Local activities are provided across the United States for Sunshine Kids of all ages and their families and include a variety of exciting opportunities
Hospital Programs
Our in-hospital programs bring sunshine and smiles to children with cancer inside the hospital environment to help brighten the day
